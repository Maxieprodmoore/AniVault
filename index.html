Professional Art Studio of the month:

Collateral Damage Studios (CDS)

Collateral Damage Studios is a group of art enthusiasts who debuted on the Singapore illustration scene in 2007. Inspired by pop culture, CDS aims to promote local creative talents, produce high quality illustrations, and have plenty of fun.
Actively engaging with international doujin communities, they work closely with companies and agencies to create concept art and branding visuals.
Reflecting on their origins in Kaleidoscope, Collateral Damage Studios advise and support student initiatives, such as Extravaganza, to provide aspiring Singaporean artists with platforms to debut.

For some examples of their work, check out their website here.
